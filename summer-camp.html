Summer Camp

Love LEGO? Dig designing? Excel at engineering? Announcing the 2014 Robotics Summer Camp hosted by the award-winning students of FIRST Robotics Competition team Code Orange.

Learn to use a proven engineering design process and advanced engineering and designing skills to make the best LEGO Mindstorms robots ever, while engaging in team building games, specialty classes, engineering challenges and, of course, snacks. The skills learned during the week will be put to the test on Friday with the "Ship Wreck Cove" FIRST LEGO League style competition and Open House.

Who: Kids age 8-14 years old

When: We are expanding!

  • Week One: Mornings, July 21-25, 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Week Two: Afternoons, July 28- August 1, 2:00 PM - 6:00 PM

Where: Code Orange Headquarters at Lasergraphics, 20 Ada, Irvine

Cost: Only $150. Includes a cool T-shirt, daily take-home surprises and lots of fun.
Checks made to Code Orange should be mailed to Code Orange, 24861 Danafir, Dana Point, CA 92629, to complete your registration. Thank You.

We will only take 18 students per session, so sign up today (and then tell your friends!)
